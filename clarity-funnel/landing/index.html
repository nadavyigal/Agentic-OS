  1. Copy the skill folder to ~/.cursor/skills/clarity-funnel/ (or .cursor/skills/clarity-funnel/ in a project).
  2. Ensure SKILL.md and profiles/ are inside that folder.
  3. In Cursor, invoke clarity-funnel or say: "Run Clarity Funnel on this paste."
