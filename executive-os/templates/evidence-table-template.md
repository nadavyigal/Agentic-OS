## Source Type Key

- Primary (first-party data, official docs, direct measurement)
- Secondary (analyst, reputable press, well-sourced article)
- Community (Reddit, forums, social — anecdotal)
- Vendor (marketing material — treat claims with caution)
- Internal (our own analytics / notes)

## Notes

- If a claim has no source, it is an assumption — move it to the assumptions section
  of the brief, not the facts.
- Record contradictions explicitly; do not hide disconfirming evidence.
