### Full description:

```
Paste any job posting. Resumely scores your resume against ATS filters and shows exactly what to fix — section by section. Apply AI-powered edits, pick a clean template, and export a job-ready PDF in minutes.

TAILOR EVERY APPLICATION
Most resume rejections happen before a recruiter reads your work. Resumely catches the specific blockers — missing keywords, weak impact bullets, unclear summary targeting — and tells you exactly what to rewrite for the role you're applying to.

ATS-AWARE FROM THE GROUND UP
Applicant tracking systems filter out resumes with parse-breaking formatting, inconsistent structure, and role-language mismatches. Resumely knows how they work. Every suggestion is tied to the specific job description you provide, not generic advice.

WHAT YOU DO IN RESUMELY
• Upload your existing resume (PDF)
• Paste the job description you're targeting
• See your ATS compatibility score by section
• Apply AI-powered section edits: summary, bullets, skills
• Choose from clean, ATS-safe templates
• Export a tailored PDF — ready to send

EXPERT ANALYSIS ON DEMAND
Need a deeper look? Expert mode gives you targeted analysis of any resume section — written at a professional hiring-manager level, with specific rewrites you can apply immediately.

DESIGN THAT DOESN'T BREAK PARSING
Resumely's templates are built to pass ATS parsing and look polished to a human reviewer. Switch between categories, preview how your resume renders, and export — all from your phone.

FREE TO USE
All core features are free. Upload resumes, run ATS checks, apply AI edits, and export tailored PDFs at no cost.

WHAT RESUMELY IS NOT
Not a generic resume template app. Not a one-size-fits-all builder. Not a subscription. Resumely is a focused workspace for job seekers who are actively applying and want a targeted resume for each specific role — fast, on mobile, without a laptop.
```
