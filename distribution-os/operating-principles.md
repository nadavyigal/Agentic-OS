# Operating Principles

Non-negotiables for distribution work. Any agent or workflow that violates one of these should stop and surface it.

## Principle 1 — Product-Led First

Every distribution asset should make the product more useful, more discoverable, or more credible. If an asset only exists to win attention, reject it.

## Principle 2 — Free and Low-Cost Channels Before Paid

Default channels are SEO, ASO, programmatic content, free tools, LinkedIn founder updates, directories, partnerships, lifecycle email. Paid ads enter the plan only after a free channel proves repeatably.

## Principle 3 — One Hypothesis Per Experiment

Every experiment has one falsifiable claim, one metric, one time box. Bundle nothing.

## Principle 4 — One Product In Focus Per Week

RunSmart and ResumeBuilder do not both get a weekly push. One gets focus, the other gets maintenance. The command center says which is which.

## Principle 5 — Founder Approves Every External Publish

Agents prepare. Founder publishes. Drafts are labeled `draft`, `reviewed`, or `approved` in the filename header. No external send, post, or deploy without explicit "yes, publish this" in the current message.

## Principle 6 — Source Of Truth Discipline

| Fact | Lives In |
|---|---|
| Product positioning | Project repo `.agent-os/distribution/product-positioning.md` |
| Channel strategy per product | Project repo `.agent-os/distribution/channels.md` |
| This week's plan and status | Notion Distribution Command Center |
| Experiment backlog and results | Notion Experiment Backlog |
| Approved assets, exports, research | Google Drive |
| Workflows, prompts, lessons | This folder (`distribution-os/`) |
| Marketing skills | `marketingskills/skills/` |

If two sources disagree, the project repo wins for product facts; Notion wins for status; Drive wins for documents; this folder wins for workflows.

## Principle 7 — Disciplined Cadence

- **Weekly**: full distribution cycle for the focused product
- **Daily**: 5-minute check on running experiments and inbox for partnerships
- **Monthly**: strategy review, channel re-scoring, kill list

The cadence is the system. Skip it and the system stops working.

## Principle 8 — Evidence For Every Claim

Performance claims (`this worked`, `this failed`) must reference a number in `metrics-dashboard.md` or a logged metric in Notion. No vibes.

## Principle 9 — Reuse Before Creation

Before writing a new asset, check Google Drive for existing drafts and the project repo for existing positioning. Most distribution work is repurposing, not invention.

## Principle 10 — Lessons Compound

Anything that worked twice or failed twice goes in `lessons.md`. Anything cross-product also goes in `~/.claude/LEARNINGS.md`.

## Principle 11 — No Manufactured Community

No fake reviews, no astroturfing, no bot accounts, no review-for-review trades. Community research is observation only. Partnerships are real relationships.

## Principle 12 — Cheap Reversibility

Prefer experiments that can be paused or rolled back in under an hour. A landing page that can be unpublished beats a programmatic SEO push that can't be cleanly removed.
